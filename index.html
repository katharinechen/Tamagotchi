Instructions

The purpose of the game is to keep your tamagotchi alive.
  1. To keep your tamagotchi alive you must keep your life points for all three categories above 0.
  2. Your life points will automatically start decreasing immediately after you submit a name for your Tamagotchi
  3. To raise your life points for each category, you might click on the corresponding circle.
  4. Click quickly!
